VS Live is a Vicious Syndicate service that provides in depth information about the current state of
the Hearthstone ladder and meta. Use the [i] button to get a starter guide to the app.

We are relying on game data provided by players of all ranks and skills. More data allows us to improve our accuracy
and provides you with more in depth game analysis. Signing up on VS also unlocks additional app functionality such
as more archetypes shown in the Table section or filtering by ranks. Signing up is easy and free.

Vicious Syndicate has pioneered rigorous data collection procedures and data analytics in Hearthstone. Our
weekly Data Reaper Report was launched in May 2016, and has been published on a consistent basis since then.
Our goal is to engage the community by collaborating on both collecting game data as well as analyzing it,
producing high-quality rigorous articles. Our content is meant for increasing the community’s collective
knowledge about Hearthstone.

For more about how we collect and analyze data [check out our FAQ].
